Class “TouchScreenDevices”

Attributes: 
1. name of devices
2. type of devices (phone, tablet)
3. price
4. date of manufacture
5. screen size
6. brand of devices
7. model of devices
8. the increased screen sensitivity

States: 
1. working device
2. broken device

Methods part 1: 
1. get the increased screen sensitivity
2. set screen size
3. get type of device

Methods part 2: 
1. get price of devices
2. get name of devices
3. set brand of devices
4. set model of devices
5. get date of manufacture

Rules: 
1. Methods part 1 are available for class “Device” to be executed.
2. Methods part 2 are internal and available only inside “TouchScreenDevices” class.
