Class “TouchScreenDevices”

Attributes: 
1. name of devices
2. type of devices (phone, tablet)
3. price
4. date of manufacture
5. screen size
6. brand of devices
7. model of devices
8. the increased screen sensitivity

States: 
1. working device
2. broken device

Methods part 1: 
1. get single player entertainments
2. get team entertainments

Methods part 2: 
1. get place of the entertainment
2. get name of the entertainment
3. get kind of the entertainment
4. know price 
5. get date of the entertainment
6. set time
 
Rules: 
1. Methods part 1 are available for class “Device” to be executed.
2. Methods part 2 are internal and available only inside “TouchScreenDevices” class.
